Blood Pressure Forecast

Forecasts blood pressure variability over time. Persistent spikes above 140/90 mmHg damage the delicate filtering units (glomeruli) within the kidneys.

Kidney Filtration (Creatinine) Forecast

Forecasts serum creatinine load. An upward trajectory indicates imminent kidney filtration failure, visualizing potential long-term kidney decline.

eGFR Trajectory

Forecasts Estimated Glomerular Filtration Rate (eGFR). A downward trend indicates disease progression. A drop below 15 indicates kidney failure.

Specific Gravity

Forecasts the kidney's ability to concentrate urine. Drifting towards 1.010 (isosthenuria) suggests the tubules have lost the ability to reabsorb water.
